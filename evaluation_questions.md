# A pilot evaluation questionnaire was developed for future user studies.

Use a 1–5 scale where 1 = strongly disagree and 5 = strongly agree.

1. The guided question encouraged me to recall what I already knew.
2. The hint supported my thinking without giving away the answer too early.
3. The self-explanation prompt helped me organize my understanding.
4. The reflection prompt helped me identify what I did and did not understand.
5. Delaying the final answer encouraged me to think before relying on AI.
6. The interaction required more active participation than a typical chatbot.
7. I would use a study assistant with this interaction style in a real course.
8. The learning sequence felt clear and easy to follow.

## Short-answer questions

1. Which feature helped your learning the most, and why?
2. Which part was frustrating or unnecessary?
3. Did you feel less likely to copy the answer without thinking? Explain.
4. What should be improved?
